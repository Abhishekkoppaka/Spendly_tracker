Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects information that you voluntarily provide while using the Service. This includes your account details (such as email address and password), expense entries (category, amount, date, description), and usage data necessary for providing and improving the Service.

We do not collect information from third parties about your financial history or spending habits. All data in Spendly is entered by you.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data you provide is used exclusively for the purpose of helping you track and understand your personal expenses. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Create and maintain your personal account
  • Display your expense summaries and category breakdowns
  • Enable filtering and analysis of your spending by date range
  • Send service-related notifications (account alerts, password resets)
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve the Service

We do not use your personal financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the Service to you.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your expense data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS). Access to your data is restricted to your authenticated session only.

While we implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong, unique passwords for your account.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services to support its operation, such as hosting providers and analytics tools. These services may have limited access to anonymized or aggregated data required for their technical functions.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personally identifiable information to any third party. Any third-party services we use are selected based on their privacy practices and are bound by data processing agreements.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data at any time through your account settings. If you choose to delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed from our servers.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at support@spendly.app.
